Data Privacy Act of 2012

Your personal data will be collected and processed in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012. This ensures your information is protected, kept confidential, and used only for legitimate purposes such as healthcare appointments, consultations, and barangay health services. You have the right to access, correct, and withdraw your data at any time.
